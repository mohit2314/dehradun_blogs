About Dehradun
Special title treatment

The capital of Uttarakhand, Dehradun is a gateway to some of the most popular hill stations in the country like Nainital and Mussoorie. Nestled in the Doon Valley, it is primarily known for the number of elite boarding schools it has like the Doon School and the Indian Military Academy. In fact, Dehradun is also called the ‘school capital of India’ for the number of schools it is home to.
